Technigala 18F

Technigala is the DALI Lab's termly showcase featuring student projects in tech and design. I was asked to create the poster for 18F (Fall of 2018). Past designs incorporated a robot character, so I continued this trend in an autumn setting and used WALL-E as a color/atmosphere inspiration. The robot represents the DALI student, each glowing blue orb step represents a different week in the term. The steps lead to the futuristic city, which represents the culminating Technigala showcase.
