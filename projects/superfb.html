Play the game here!

Over my Junior Fall, I worked on a team at DALI Lab to help a professor develop the course CS 66: Game Development. We collaborated on constructing the curriculum and creating a sample runner game in Unity whose assets could be used by students to create their own simple runner game for Lab 1.

I worked with the project manager to understand the principles of game design and created the assets for what eventually became Super Frat Bros, a parody on Dartmouth culture.
