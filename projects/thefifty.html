Each fall, spring and summer, students embark on the legendary "Fifty", a 53 mile non-stop hike from Dartmouth's Moosilauke Lodge in Warren, NH to Robinson Hall on campus. The journey usually takes 24-36 hours, and many student volunteers set up camp along the way to support the weary hikers. Upon completion, hikers and supporters of the Fall 2016 Fifty all received this sticker!
