The Dartmouth Climbing Team is a new competitive climbing club that began in 2016, and I was tasked with creating a logo for the team. We decided that something "sharp" and "blocky" would evoke the strength and bouldering aspects of the team, and we wanted to incorporate the Dartmouth green color as well as various grays on a black background. After some more discussion with the team captains, I came up with a silhouetted climber atop a blocky Baker Berry tower that symbolizes an indoor bouldering wall. For stickers and t-shirt designs, I created a climbing shoe version as well as the original logo in the center of exploding "rocks".
