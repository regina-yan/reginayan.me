Sexual Assault Awareness Month

I created this logo for Sexual Assault Awareness Month, which is our Movement Against Violence's annual awareness campaign. I wanted to create a simple logo that would incorporate the movement’s teal ribbon with Dartmouth’s Lone Pine logo and came up with this design. I chose a slightly doodle-y feel for the Lone Pine logo which gives a more friendly, authentic feel to the sticker.
