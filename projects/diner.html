Diner Maya Model

My CS 22 class spent four weeks modeling a room in Maya. I decided to create a diner, which takes various aspects of my favorite high school locations (such as the retro games from my ice rink, diner tables from Winstead’s, etc).

I used mental ray for the rendering, and hopefully if I have some time in the future, I will re-render the scene using Maya’s new Arnold Renderer which creates much more realistic-looking shots! I would also work on the texturing because that can definitely be improved. Either way, I am proud of my work, and below are some screenshots of the room renderings!
