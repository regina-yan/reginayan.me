Collis Governing Board asked me to create a poster for Collis Cabaret, a show that features faculty and student performers. I designed this poster in Illustrator and am proud of how the objects and shadows turned out.

TAKEAWAYS

I really wanted to build on color theory lessons I learned from my painting class and use lighting as a way to push myself. I think I accomplished that here, especially with the chair. Moving forwards I'll definitely think about lighting more and how different kinds of lighting can evoke different moods.
