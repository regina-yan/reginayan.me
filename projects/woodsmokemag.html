Woodsmoke 2019

The Dartmouth Outing Club (DOC) is home to the nation's oldest student-run outdoors club. It's a pretty incredible community with groups for everyone interested in getting outside, from the novice kayaker to the experienced boulderer.

For Dartmouth's 250th anniversary, I designed and published Woodsmoke 2019, a celebration of the amazing explorations of folks in the DOC from 2017-2019. Special thanks to Rory Gawler '05 for supporting me throughout this process and to the Outdoor Programs Office for their dedication to getting us outside.
