Women in the Wilderness, or “WIW”, is a female-led sub club of the Dartmouth Outing Club. The club was created in 1992 but did not have many members and fizzled out. In 2015 Carolyn McShea revived the club and asked me to help design the logo. This design is used on t-shirts and stickers for the club, which now has over 60 members!
