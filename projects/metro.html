Ode to Metro

I moved back to the DC area in fall 2021 and wanted to make some barstools for our new home. I was inspired by the ups and downs of my metro experiences and created Ode to Metro: Metro Minimal, a neat and tidy series of strips, and Metro Mayhem, a mess of blobs (just like the red line)!
Some behind the scenes shots with my homemade frame and my attempt at drilling things together without a work table
