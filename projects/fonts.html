reggie
This is my handwriting and I turned it into a font! I've kerned the letters as well as I could, but please let me know if you see areas for improvement. The story is me asking ChatGPT: "give me a 300 character fairytale story"
LILY THE KIND
Once upon a time, in a far-off land, there was a kind-hearted peasant girl named Lily. One day, while wandering in the woods, she stumbled upon a magical fairy who granted her three wishes. Excitedly, Lily wished for a never-ending supply of food, a beautiful dress, and a kind and loving husband. Her wishes were granted, and she lived happily ever after, sharing her abundance with others in need. However, Lily soon realized that her greatest wish had been the gift of kindness from the fairy, and she made a vow to spread kindness wherever she went. And so, her legend grew as the kind-hearted Lily, a true fairy tale hero.

And now a block font! This one is much better as a display font than a text type as you'll see below when I asked ChatGPT "give me a 300 character feminist fairytale story"
LEILA THE FEMINIST
Once upon a time, in a kingdom far away, a princess named Leila was born. Despite being the daughter of the king, she refused to be confined to traditional gender roles and instead trained to become a skilled warrior. When an evil sorcerer threatened her kingdom, Leila bravely led her army to victory, proving that women were just as capable as men. However, after the battle, Leila was met with opposition from those who believed that women should stay at home and tend to domestic duties. Refusing to give in, Leila worked to change societal expectations and became a champion for women's rights. She went on to become the first queen to rule the kingdom alone, inspiring countless girls to believe in themselves and their abilities. And so, the legend of Queen Leila, a true feminist fairy tale hero, was born.

Now for a 3D block font, this time asking ChatGPT "can you make the feminism more subtle"
ELLA THE EMPATH
Once upon a time, there was a princess named Ella who loved nothing more than to explore the woods surrounding her castle. One day, she stumbled upon a group of woodsmen who were being forced to work in the kingdom's mines against their will. Determined to help, Ella used her royal influence to free the woodsmen and help them start new lives. Her actions sparked a change in the kingdom, as more and more people began to see the value in treating all individuals with kindness and respect, regardless of their gender or station in life. And so, Ella's legacy as a compassionate and empathetic ruler, who worked to create a more equitable society, was born.
