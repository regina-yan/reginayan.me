BrewHaHa Stickers

Each spring, the Dartmouth Organic Farm throws the zero-waste event "BrewHaHa" featuring local food, brews, and live music. I was asked to created a design that could be used on stickers and t-shirts, which you can see here. I do wish I had better materials when I designed this (used my finger on a friend's iPad mini screen, this was before I had access to/learned Adobe Suite), so the line quality and general smoothness could be better. Still, I'm very happy with how it turned out. Eventually I also created a "Harfest" sticker, which is the Organic Farm's fall version of this event!
