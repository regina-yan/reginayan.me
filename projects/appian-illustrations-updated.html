Appian Product Illustrations (Rebrand!)

A year after introducing product illustrations to the Appian platform, our company went through a full rebrand. This rebrand included a new style guide for illustrations -- oof, a lot of illustrations to update! I worked with the marketing team to push the direction of the new style to continue embodying the values we had in the initial illustrations, and I continued leading our platform illustrations team to rebrand and improve upon our existing graphics.
