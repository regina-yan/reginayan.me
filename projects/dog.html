The Dartmouth Outing Guide contains information on outdoors activities around the Dartmouth area including white water kayaking rivers, sport climbing locations, and hiking routes. The Dartmouth Outing Club asked me to create designs for the Guide's new cover, and I came up with these two ideas. This first design is minimalistic in feel with contrasting orange and green colors to give the cover some pop and lightheartedness. The background is a paper texture (a bit difficult to see in this photo) and the letters give it feel of paper cut-outs. The second design was inspired by Firewatch and features scenery from the Appalachian Trail.
