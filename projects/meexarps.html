Meexarps

Franklin Zheng approached me in 2020 to help design a game he's been developing: Meexarps, a multi-player social game where players answer questions about themselves and try to match up everyone's answers to the individual. Franklin had the idea and the dev power, and he needed someone to design the user experience and graphics. A year and a lot of hard work later, we launched Meexarps on AirConsole.
