I created these stickers my freshman spring for Big Green Dreams, a campaign launched by Dartmouth Sustainability’s EcoReps to promote sustainability and "green dreams" for the College.

TAKEAWAYS

This was my first real project and the launching point for my design path at college. I stumbled upon this opportunity when a friend who knew about my doodling habits recommended me to the EcoReps. I had never created designs before and did not have the software or hardware to so, but thanks to my roommate’s iPad and lots of patience (fingers are quite imprecise), I started drawing my ideas. Overall, I really learned how to push myself and never turn down a project, because in this case it led me to more opportunities that brought me to where I am today.
