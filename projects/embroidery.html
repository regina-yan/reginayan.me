Embroidery

With all the travelling, car camping and moving-around, I needed a small portable project that could go through TSA in a carry-on bag (and unfortunately my punch needle tools don't make the cut). Embroidery to the rescue! Here's a photo dump of keychains and clothes, all gifts :)


Shirts
Fun gifts for friends and family! The Dartmouth sweatshirts are made with felt that I hand-cut and embroidered onto clothing. Might look into machine embroidery in the future because these actually took forever.
Patches
Fun and super portable, but need to do a better job with finishing the edges
Patches, on hats!
Same comment about finishing the edges (it's especially bad on my first attempt, rock climbing Keggy). Used Fabric Fuse tape to stick them on the hats.
