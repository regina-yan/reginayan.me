Nomad

2023 was a year of digital nomading along the west coast. First stop? San Francisco! My partner Shep and I were lucky enough to find a Facebook Marketplace sublet on Ocean Beach with a large window overlooking the ocean. Working early east coast hours, I watched gorgeous sunrise after sunrise as the sky transformed into countless shades of purple, pink and blue. I also happened to live near Love Fest Fibers who offers small-batch, sustainably sourced yarn and my goodness I'm never going back to Joann's again! (my wallet disagrees).

I present to you, Nomad!
Look at how fun the pokey yellow yarn is! It's called "Color Core"
...and, of course, the view from the window! (yes, this is a sunset view, but the photos of the wall hanging are from sunrise, don't worry!)
