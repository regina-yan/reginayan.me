About Me

empathetic, resilient, goofy

Much of who I am is based on my love for understanding people, my grit and determination to learn anything I set my mind to, and my light-hearted, silly attitude towards life.

I am currently an Associate UX Designer at Appian and recently graduated from Dartmouth College with a major in Engineering modified with Studio Art and a minor in Human-Centered Design. I'm primarily interested in product and UI/UX design and am inspired by solutions that positively impact our community and environment. I am so thankful for all the opportunities I have had and cannot wait for what's next.
